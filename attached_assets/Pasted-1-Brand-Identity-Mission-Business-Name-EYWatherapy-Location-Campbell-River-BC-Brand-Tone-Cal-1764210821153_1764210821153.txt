3. Visual Style & Design Guidelines
Aesthetic

Nature-inspired, calm, coastal Vancouver Island tones

Warm, clean, and modern layout

Avoid clinical “hospital white” — opt for comfort-forward design

Colour Palette (Suggested)

Deep forest green

Soft ocean blue

Warm sand/beige neutrals

Gentle white/cream backgrounds

Typography

Headings: modern smooth serif OR clean soft sans-serif

Body text: simple, readable sans-serif

Round, friendly line spacing (not cramped)

Image Style (Original, Not Copied)

Warm natural lighting

Massage therapy scenes with soft-focus atmosphere

Abstract nature textures (wood grain, ocean water, ferns)

Optional lifestyle shots to evoke ease, breathing, grounding

UI/UX Essentials

Clear buttons and easy-to-find booking links

Good mobile responsiveness

Avoid clutter — prioritize calm space and readable content

Soft animations (fade-ins, gentle motion) acceptable but minimal
